What To Do If Your Vehicle Breaks Down

Stay calm and follow these simple steps while waiting for roadside assistance.

⚠️

Stay Safe

Turn on your hazard lights immediately and move your vehicle to a safe location if possible.

📍

Know Your Location

Identify your nearest landmark or share your live location with the towing operator.

📞

Contact an Operator

Browse nearby operators on Auto-on-Demand and discuss pricing before the tow begins.

🚚

Wait Safely

Stay clear of moving traffic and wait in a safe place until your towing operator arrives.
